The HIT Constitution

The 10 Laws.

These aren't suggestions. They are the necessary conditions for survival in the age of Artificial Agency.

I

AI is not a Tool. It is Labor.

Stop treating AI like a calculator. Treat it like a deaf, indefatigable employee. If you don't give it a job title, a description, and KPIs, it will fail.

"Use ChatGPT to write" "Hire a Content Agent"
II

No Responsibility without Control.

An agent cannot own a P&L. The human ("Domain Lead") is always responsible for the agent's output. Agents scale execution, not blame.

III

Data is Fuel, Process is the Engine.

A powerful AI model on poor data is useless. A mediocre model on excellent data is magic. Clean the pipes before flowing the water.

IV

Hybrid > Pure.

100% automation is fragile. 100% human is slow. The resilient system is 80% machine (speed) and 20% human (judgment and exceptions).

V

Codify Tribal Knowledge.

What exists in Mario's head must become a Python script. If a process depends on undocumented intuition, it is not scalable.
