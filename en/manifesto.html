The 10 Laws.

This is not a user manual. It is the genetic code of HIT organizations.

I

Law of Symbiosis

Humans direct, AI executes. Never the reverse. AI must never set strategic goals without human supervision. Humans must never perform repetitive tasks that can be algorithimicized.

Violation: Human Burnout or Strategic Hallucination.

II

Law of Sovereignty

Data is your blood. Don't donate it. Organizational knowledge must reside on infrastructure controlled by the company, encrypted and isolated from public models.

Violation: Vendor Lock-in, Data Leakage.

III

Law of Codification

If it's not code, it's opinion. Every operational process must be transformed into executable workflows (n8n, Python). "Tribal knowledge" is the enemy of scale.

IV

Law of Asynchronous Speed

Work doesn't wait for meetings. Agents work 24/7. Humans interact when necessary. The system must be designed to be asynchronous by default.

V

Law of Radical Transparency

Every AI action must be logged. A "Black Box" is not admissible in business. We must always be able to answer the question: "Why did the Agent do this?".
