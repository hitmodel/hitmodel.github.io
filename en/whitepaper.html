Security Protocol.

1. Data Sovereignty & GDPR
All data resides on European servers you own (Hetzner/OVH) or on-premise. No data ever leaves the EU. Full GDPR compliance by design, with immutable access logs.
2. RBAC & Zero Trust
Role-Based Access Control at every level. AI agents have minimum necessary permissions (Least Privilege Principle). Every API interaction is authenticated and encrypted.
3. Audit Logs
An immutable registry of every action performed by agents and humans. Who did what, and when. Essential for financial and legal compliance.
