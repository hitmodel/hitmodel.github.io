Vision & Philosophy

The Manifesto
HIT.

Artificial intelligence is not a tool to be added to existing teams. It is a new form of cognitive capability that makes obsolete the organizational models upon which modern work has been built.

Continuing to treat AI as marginal support means replicating old inefficiencies with more powerful technology. The real limitation is not AI, but the human structures that surround it.

"The future of work is not about adopting AI, but designing natively hybrid teams."

Hybrid Intelligence Teams are born from the intentional fusion of domain expertise and artificial intelligence expertise. They are not "AI-enabled" teams, but AI-native teams, where people and intelligent systems collaborate as a single cognitive unit.

In this model, AI does not replace human judgment nor blindly automate processes: it expands the team's ability to understand complexity, explore possibilities, and make higher-quality decisions.

We believe that the competitive advantage of the future will not derive from the size of organizations nor from the amount of technology adopted, but from the quality of collective intelligence that teams are able to generate.

Those who adopt this model will not simply use AI better than others: they will work in a fundamentally different way.
