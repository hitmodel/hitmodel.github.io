## ðŸŽ¯ Key Concepts

### What is a HIT?

A **Hybrid Intelligence Team** is an organizational unit designed to operate in an AI-native context, where collaboration between humans and AI systems is not an addition but the founding principle of the structure itself.

### Core Pillars

1. **Domain Expertise**: Deep knowledge of a sector, market, or operational context
2. **AI Expertise**: Advanced understanding of AI models, their limitations, and how to orchestrate human-AI interaction

### Key Roles

- **Domain Lead**: Expert with deep sector knowledge who guides problem definition
- **AI Lead**: Architect of cognitive flows who orchestrates AI systems
- **Hybrid Operators**: Professionals operating at the intersection of domain and AI
